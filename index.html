Galaxy Overview

Our Home in the Cosmos

The Milky Way is a barred spiral galaxy 100,000 light-years across containing 100 - 400 billion stars. Our Solar System orbits in the Orion Arm, 26,000 light-years from the centre.

Fascinating Facts

Things You Need to Know

Spiral Structure

The Milky Way is a barred spiral galaxy with four sweeping arms extending from its central bar.

Galactic Halo

A vast, invisible halo of dark matter surrounds the Milky Way and contains extremely ancient stellar populations.

Star Formation

Around one or two new stars form each year inside dense molecular clouds known as stellar nurseries.

High-Speed Orbit

Our Solar System travels at around 220 km/s through the Milky Way, completing a full galactic orbit every 225 - 250 million years.
